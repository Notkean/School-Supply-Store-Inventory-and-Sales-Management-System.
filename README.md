School Supply Store Inventory and Sales Management System

Description

The School Supply Store Inventory and Sales Management System is a CRUD-based application designed to help school supply stores efficiently manage their inventory, customer information, suppliers, and sales transactions. The system provides a centralized platform for tracking available products, recording sales, monitoring stock levels, and managing supplier records to improve business operations.

Problem Statement

Many school supply stores still rely on notebooks or spreadsheets to record inventory and sales transactions. This manual process often leads to inaccurate stock counts, misplaced records, delayed sales reporting, and difficulty monitoring product availability. The proposed School Supply Store Inventory and Sales Management System provides a digital solution that allows staff to manage products, customers, suppliers, and sales efficiently while automatically updating inventory after each completed sale.


Features

* 📦 Manage Product Inventory (Create, Read, Update, Delete)
* 👥 Manage Customer Information
* 🚚 Manage Supplier Records
* 🛒 Record and Manage Sales Transactions
* 📉 Automatically Update Product Stock After Sales
* 📊 Dashboard for Inventory and Sales Monitoring
