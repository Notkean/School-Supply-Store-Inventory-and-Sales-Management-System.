School Supply Store Inventory and Sales Management System

Description

The School Supply Store Inventory and Sales Management System is a CRUD-based application designed to help school supply stores efficiently manage their inventory, customer information, suppliers, and sales transactions. The system provides a centralized platform for tracking available products, recording sales, monitoring stock levels, and managing supplier records to improve business operations.
